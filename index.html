About

Sailesh Patel is a junior Front-End Engineer at Purplebricks, based in Birmingham, UK with a Computing Science degree from Aston University.

Sailesh has worked for companies like Kainos, and Majestic, organised and run developer events like AstonHack 2017, and has given talks at events like MLH Hackcon EU III, where he spoke about supporting neurodiverse participants.

Sailesh has experience with web technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript/TypeScript (including ReactJS, VueJS, & jQuery), as well as backend technologies like Java, and NodeJS.

To get in touch with Sailesh, you can find him on LinkedIn, Polywork, GitHub, Mastodon, and Dev.to.
